=== "中文"

#### 微积分

- **主要概念**：极限、导数、积分等。
- **学习要点**：掌握求导和积分的基本技巧以及相关定理。

#### 线性代数

- **主要概念**：向量与矩阵、线性变换、特征值与特征向量、矩阵分解等。
- **学习要点**：理解矩阵运算及其几何意义，掌握常用分解方法。

#### 概率与统计

- **主要概念**：随机变量、概率分布、期望与方差、假设检验等。
- **学习要点**：熟悉常见分布及其性质，能够应用基础统计方法分析数据。

=== "English"

#### Calculus

- **Key Concepts**: limits, derivatives, integrals and related ideas.
- **Study Tips**: Focus on mastering basic differentiation and integration techniques as well as important theorems.

#### Linear Algebra

- **Key Concepts**: vectors and matrices, linear transformations, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, matrix decompositions, etc.
- **Study Tips**: Understand matrix operations and their geometric meaning, and learn common decomposition methods.

#### Probability & Statistics

- **Key Concepts**: random variables, probability distributions, expectation and variance, hypothesis testing, and so on.
- **Study Tips**: Become familiar with common distributions and their properties, and practice applying basic statistical methods to data analysis.
