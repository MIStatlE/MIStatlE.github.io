# 心得感悟

学习过程中积累的感悟和经验可以帮助我们不断改进方法。本页用于记录个人在自学数学、机器学习、算法和人工智能时的体会。

## 学习策略

- 制定合理的学习计划，分阶段完成学习目标；
- 及时复习巩固知识，避免遗忘；
- 多动手实践，通过项目加深理解。

## 心理建设

- 保持好奇心和耐心，遇到困难时不要气馁；
- 学会与他人交流，共同讨论能解决很多问题；
- 注意劳逸结合，保证充足休息。

## 反思记录

你可以根据实际情况撰写反思与总结，记录哪些学习方法有效、哪些需要调整，形成个人的知识体系。

=== "English"

# Reflections

Experiences and insights accumulated during learning can help us constantly improve our methods. This page is used to record personal reflections while self‑learning mathematics, machine learning, algorithms and artificial intelligence.

## Study strategies

- Make reasonable study plans and complete learning goals in stages;
- Review and consolidate knowledge in time to avoid forgetting;
- Practice a lot; deepen understanding through projects.

## Mental health

- Keep curiosity and patience; do not get discouraged when encountering difficulties;
- Learn to communicate with others; discussing together can solve many problems;
- Maintain a balance between work and rest and ensure adequate rest.

## Reflection log

You can write reflections and summaries according to actual conditions and record which learning methods are effective and which need adjustment to form your personal knowledge system.
