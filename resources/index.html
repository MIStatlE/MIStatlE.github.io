    推荐资源#

    本页收集了在自学数学、机器学习、算法和人工智能过程中常用的资源。不同人对资源的偏好不同，因此这里提供一个框架和示例，便于您根据自身需要进行扩充。

    数学基础#

    • 教科书：例如高等数学、线性代数、概率论与统计等经典教材。
    • 课程：可选择大学开放课程（如公开课或在线课程平台），系统学习基础概念。
    • 参考书：例如《线性代数及其应用》《概率论与随机过程》等。

    机器学习#

    • 经典教材：如《机器学习》（周志华）或《Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning》。
    • 在线课程：选择知名高校开设的机器学习课程，自主安排学习进度。
    • 实践项目：通过动手实践小项目，加深对算法的理解。

    算法设计#

    • 基础书籍：如《算法导论》《算法设计手册》。
    • 题目练习：在各类在线评测平台上刷题，提高算法设计与分析能力。
    • 课程资源：查找计算机专业院校的算法课程公开资料。

    人工智能#

    • 概念性书籍：例如《人工智能：一种现代的方法》，介绍智能系统的基本原理。
    • 专题资源：针对自然语言处理、计算机视觉、强化学习等领域的专门课程与论文。
    • 社区与会议：关注顶级会议和期刊，了解最新发展。

    以上仅为示例条目，可根据个人情况补充更多资源，并记录学习过程中的心得。

    Recommended resources#

    This page collects commonly used resources in the process of self‑learning mathematics, machine learning, algorithms and artificial intelligence. People have different preferences for resources, so here we provide a framework and examples so that you can expand according to your own needs.

    Mathematics#

    • Textbooks: e.g., calculus, linear algebra, probability and statistics.
    • Courses: choose open university courses (such as OCW or online platforms) to systematically learn basic concepts.
    • References: e.g., “Linear Algebra and Its Applications”, “Probability and Random Processes”, etc.

    Machine learning#

    • Classic textbooks: such as “Machine Learning” (Zhihua Zhou) or “Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning”.
    • Online courses: choose machine learning courses offered by well‑known universities; arrange your own schedule.
    • Projects: By hands‑on practice with small projects, deepen your understanding of algorithms.

    Algorithm design#

    • Basic books: e.g., “Introduction to Algorithms”, “The Algorithm Design Manual”.
    • Problem solving: practice on online judges to improve algorithm design and analysis skills.
    • Course materials: find open course resources of algorithm courses in universities.

    Artificial intelligence#

    • Concept books: such as “Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach”, introducing the basic principles of intelligent systems.
    • Specialized resources: courses and papers focusing on natural language processing, computer vision, reinforcement learning, etc.
    • Community & conferences: follow top conferences and journals to stay up to date.

    The above are sample entries; you can supplement more resources according to your personal situation and record your insights.
