# Advanced Mathematics

After mastering the basics, you can move on to advanced topics that are useful for deeper understanding of algorithms, machine learning and AI.

## Numerical Optimization

- Gradient descent, Newton’s method, conjugate gradient and other optimization algorithms;
- Function convexity, Lagrange multipliers and constrained optimization.

## Probabilistic Graphical Models

- Bayesian networks, Markov random fields and related structures;
- Inference and learning algorithms such as the forward‑backward algorithm and the EM algorithm.

## Information Theory & Statistical Learning

- Concepts like entropy, mutual information and KL divergence;
- Maximum likelihood estimation, Bayesian inference and decision theory.

## Other Areas

You may also study functional analysis, differential geometry or group theory, depending on your research interests.

Feel free to expand this chapter based on your needs, and always tie these concepts back to practical applications.
