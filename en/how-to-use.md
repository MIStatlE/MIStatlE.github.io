# How to Use This Guide

The guide is divided into several themed sections. Each contains recommended resources, key points and downloadable notes (PDFs). Here's how to make the most of it:

1. **Understand the structure**: Start with the *Preface* and *CS Learning Plan* to understand the purpose and suggested order of the chapters.
2. **Jump as needed**: You can follow the table of contents step by step, or pick topics based on your interests or knowledge gaps.
3. **Upload your notes**: If you have your own study notes, you can upload them as PDF files into the relevant chapters for later reference.
4. **Use tools effectively**: Mastering version control, command‑line tools and programming environments will greatly improve your efficiency.

This guide provides only a reference path; feel free to adjust it to suit your own situation.
