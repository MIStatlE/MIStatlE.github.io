# Media

Besides sharing notes and articles on this site, I also post videos and short content on other platforms such as Bilibili, Xiaohongshu and Zhihu. This page collects these media resources and shows how to embed or link them in Markdown.

## Bilibili

You can find my videos—learning notes, course interpretations and more—on Bilibili. Here is an example of embedding a video player using an `<iframe>` tag:

```html
<iframe src="//player.bilibili.com/player.html?bvid=BV1XX411c7mD&page=1"
        scrolling="no" frameborder="no" framespacing="0" allowfullscreen>
</iframe>
```

Replace the `bvid`, `page` and other parameters with your own video ID.

## Xiaohongshu

If you post course notes or short videos on Xiaohongshu, you can add links to them here, for example:

- [My Xiaohongshu home page](https://www.xiaohongshu.com/user/your-id)
- [Sample note](https://www.xiaohongshu.com/explore/xxxxx)

Since Xiaohongshu does not provide official embed codes, sharing via links is usually sufficient.

## Zhihu

Zhihu articles or columns can be referenced with standard Markdown links, for example:

- [My Zhihu article: How to self‑learn machine learning](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/123456789)

You can also record the Q&A or columns you publish on Zhihu here.

---

If you have resources on other platforms (such as Douyin, Weibo, etc.), add them here in a similar way. By collecting media content in one place, readers can easily switch between platforms to access more forms of learning material.
