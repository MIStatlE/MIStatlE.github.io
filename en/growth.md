# Personal Growth

Self‑learning is not only about accumulating knowledge but also about developing self‑awareness and mental resilience. This chapter shares psychology books, blogs and practical tips to help you maintain a positive mindset and improve study efficiency.

## Recommended Books

- **Mindset** by Carol S. Dweck: explores the importance of a growth mindset;
- **Peak** by Anders Ericsson: explains how deliberate practice leads to expert performance;
- **Atomic Habits** by James Clear: teaches strategies for building good habits and breaking bad ones;
- **The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People** by Stephen R. Covey: a classic on personal management and leadership.

## Great Blogs

- **Wait But Why**: discusses complex topics in a friendly style;
- **LessWrong**: focuses on rational thinking and decision science;
- **Overreacted**: written by React core member Dan Abramov, covering programming and personal growth.

## Mental Regulation & Productivity

- Maintain regular sleep and moderate exercise to ensure sufficient energy;
- Set achievable goals and reward yourself for progress;
- Practice mindfulness meditation or journaling to improve focus and emotional control.

Improving self‑awareness and psychological resilience will help you stay motivated and maintain a healthy state throughout your learning journey.
