# Mathematics Basics

This chapter records foundational mathematics including calculus, linear algebra and probability. It is recommended to study each topic step by step.

## Calculus

- **Key concepts**: limits, derivatives, integrals, etc.
- **Study tips**: Master basic differentiation and integration techniques along with important theorems such as Taylor expansions and substitution.

## Linear Algebra

- **Key concepts**: vectors and matrices, linear transformations, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, matrix decompositions, etc.
- **Study tips**: Understand matrix operations and their geometric interpretations; learn common decomposition methods like eigen decomposition and SVD.

## Probability & Statistics

- **Key concepts**: random variables, probability distributions, expectation and variance, hypothesis testing, etc.
- **Study tips**: Become familiar with common distributions and their properties, and practice applying basic statistical methods to data analysis.

More advanced topics, such as optimization methods and numerical computation, can be explored in later chapters.
